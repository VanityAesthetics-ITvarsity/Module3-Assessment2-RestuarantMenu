SPAGHETTI CARBONARA


Price: R155

Serves: 2


Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish renowned for its simplicity and depth of flavor. Al dente spaghetti noodles are tossed in a luxurious sauce made from eggs, Parmesan cheese, and crispy pancetta or guanciale. The dish is finished with a generous grinding of black pepper, adding a subtle heat, and garnished with fresh parsley. Each forkful offers a creamy, savory experience that epitomizes Italian comfort cuisine at its finest.
